# My Favorite Series: Sanggre

The series tells the story of Terra, the missing daughter of Danaya, who grew up in the human world unaware of her heritage. She acts as a superhero in the human world before returning to Encantadia to face a new enemy, Queen Mitena of Mine-a .

**Genre:** fantasy drama
**Plot:**  A young girl, unknowingly the lost offspring of Sang'gre Danaya, grows up in the mortal world .
